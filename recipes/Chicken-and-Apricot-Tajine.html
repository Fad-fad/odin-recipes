Chicken and Apricot Tajine

Description

Indulge your taste buds with the exotic flavors of this Chicken and Apricot Tajine. Slow-cooked to perfection, this Moroccan-inspired dish combines succulent chicken thighs with a medley of aromatic spices, including cumin, coriander, and cinnamon. The real magic happens as the tender meat absorbs the sweet and tangy notes from dried apricots, creating a harmonious blend of savory and fruity goodness. Simmered in a rich broth and garnished with slivered almonds and fresh cilantro, this tajine promises a culinary journey that transports you straight to the vibrant streets of Marrakech. A true celebration of North African cuisine, this dish is a feast for both the senses and the soul.

Ingredients:

  • 4 chicken thighs, bone-in
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • 1 teaspoon coriander
  • 1 teaspoon cinnamon
  • 1/2 teaspoon ginger
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric
  • 1/2 cup dried apricots, halved
  • 1 cup chicken broth
  • 1 tablespoon honey
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley for garnish
  • Toasted almonds for topping

Steps:

  1. In a large tajine or skillet, brown the chicken thighs on both sides. Remove and set aside.
  2. In the same pan, sauté the onion and garlic until softened.
  3. Add the cumin, coriander, cinnamon, ginger, and turmeric. Stir well.
  4. Place the browned chicken back into the pan, add apricots, and pour in the chicken broth.
  5. Drizzle honey over the mixture, season with salt and pepper, and bring to a simmer.
  6. Cover and let it simmer on low heat for 30-35 minutes until the chicken is cooked through.
  7. Garnish with fresh parsley and toasted almonds before serving.
