Tajine without Meat aka dwaz

Description

Experience the essence of Moroccan cuisine with our Vegetable Tajine. Packed with slow-cooked veggies and aromatic spices, it's a delightful, meat-free journey through North African flavors.

Ingredients

  • 2 zucchinis, sliced
  • 2 carrots, chopped
  • 1 can chickpeas, drained
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • 1 teaspoon coriander
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric
  • 1/4 teaspoon cinnamon
  • 1 can diced tomatoes
  • 1/2 cup vegetable broth
  • 1/3 cup dried apricots, sliced
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish
  • Slivered almonds for topping

Steps

  1. In a large tajine or pot, saut√© the onion and garlic until softened.
  2. Add the zucchinis, carrots, and chickpeas, stirring to combine.
  3. Sprinkle in the cumin, coriander, paprika, turmeric, and cinnamon.
  4. Stir well.
  5. Pour in the diced tomatoes and vegetable broth, bringing the mixture to a gentle simmer.
  6. Add the dried apricots and season with salt and pepper to taste.
  7. Cover and let it simmer on low heat for 25-30 minutes, allowing the flavors to meld.
  8. Once the vegetables are tender, garnish with fresh cilantro and slivered almonds.
