Vegeterian Couscous Salad

Description

Elevate your salad game with this refreshing Vegetarian Couscous Salad. Packed with vibrant veggies, fluffy couscous, and a zesty lemon vinaigrette, it's a light and satisfying dish perfect for any occasion. The combination of cherry tomatoes, cucumber, bell peppers, and feta cheese provides a burst of colors and flavors. Topped with fresh herbs and a sprinkle of toasted pine nuts, this salad is not only visually appealing but also a delightful mix of textures. Enjoy it as a standalone meal or as a side dish at your next gathering.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup couscous
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1 cucumber, diced
  • 1 red bell pepper, chopped
  • 1/2 cup crumbled feta cheese
  • 1/4 cup fresh parsley, chopped
  • 2 tablespoons fresh mint, chopped
  • 1/4 cup pine nuts, toasted
  • 1/4 cup extra-virgin olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons lemon juice
  • 1 teaspoon Dijon mustard
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps:

  1. Cook the couscous according to package instructions. Fluff with a fork and let it cool.
  2. In a large bowl, combine the couscous, cherry tomatoes, cucumber, red bell pepper, feta cheese, parsley, and mint.
  3. In a small bowl, whisk together olive oil, lemon juice, Dijon mustard, salt, and pepper to create the vinaigrette.
  4. Pour the vinaigrette over the couscous mixture and toss until well combined.
  5. Sprinkle toasted pine nuts on top for a crunchy finish.
  6. Refrigerate for at least 30 minutes before serving to let the flavors meld.
  7. Serve chilled and relish the refreshing taste of this Vegetarian Couscous Salad.
